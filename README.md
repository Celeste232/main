## What is Meow Mode?

Meow Mode is a tiny hand-drawn cat that lives on top of your desktop. It strolls around, naps in its little house, eats from its bowls, and does over 30 different things — loafing, zoomies, grooming, making biscuits, sulking, and more.

- 🐾 **Doodle-style cat** roams your screen
- 🏠 **Its own house + food/water bowls** — drag them anywhere
- 🎨 **30+ behaviors** (loaf, zoomies, grooming, knead, headbonk, sulk…)
- 🔊 **3 synthesized meow sounds** to choose from
- 🌏 **4 languages**: 한국어 / English / 日本語 / 中文
- 💵 **$3.49**, lifetime free updates

## Download & Install

> Get it on **[Gumroad](#)** *(link coming soon)*.

1. Download the right `.dmg` for your Mac:
   - **Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4):** `Meow Mode-x.x.x-arm64.dmg`
   - **Intel:** `Meow Mode-x.x.x-x64.dmg`
   - Not sure? Apple menu →  **About This Mac** → check the chip.
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Meow Mode** into your **Applications** folder.
3. **First launch:** right-click the app → **Open** → **Open** again.
   *(macOS Sequoia: System Settings → Privacy & Security → "Open Anyway".)*
   This is needed once because the app isn't notarized — it's safe.

## Using it

- The cat does its own thing. Drag it around, drag the house, click the bowls to fill them.
- **Menu bar icon** (top-right): pause, find the cat, change layer, quit.
- **Settings:** rename the cat, activity level, roam area, sound type, language, and more.
